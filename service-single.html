Barter Constructions provides premium RCC structural casting, masonry, internal/external plastering, painting, and turnkey development services across Mumbai, Pune, Thane, and Lonavala. Under our unique real estate liquidity solution model, developers and vendors can trade services, raw materials, or contracting works directly in exchange for completed real estate flats, shops, or commercial office spaces.

For our selected category segment, we structure specific barter term sheets. Whether you are a builder seeking execution of these works or a supplier/subcontractor providing these specialized materials/works, we can structure an exchange to safeguard your liquid cash flow requirements.
