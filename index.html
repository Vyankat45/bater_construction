Barter Construction FAQs

What is real estate barter construction and how does it work?

Barter construction is an arrangement where a contractor executes construction services (such as foundation works, structural RCC, masonry, plastering, or complete turnkey building) in exchange for real estate inventory (flats, shops, offices) within the project, instead of demanding cash payments. This relieves developers of upfront capital strains.

What types of construction services are covered under barter?

Barter Constructions provides comprehensive services including heavy RCC structural casting, masonry, internal and external double-coat plastering, paint finishes, electrical/plumbing installations, turnkey lock-and-key developments, and bulk building material procurement (aggregates, steel, cement).

Which regions are served by Barter Constructions?

We actively execute projects and barter contracts across Maharashtra's key real estate markets, including South Mumbai (Opera House, Girgaon, Worli), Borivali, Thane, Pune city limits, and premium vacation spots like Lonavala.

How are flat or shop valuations determined for a barter agreement?

Valuations are mutually agreed upon during strategic consultations before signing contracts. Property units (flats/shops) are typical valued at a negotiated discount relative to standard market rates or current RERA-registered values to account for liquidity structures.

Are barter contracts legally secure and compliant under RERA?

Yes. All barter arrangements are formal contracts registered legally. Property units allocated to us are verified for clean titles, free of bank mortgages, and backed by escrow accounts. All projects comply strictly with Maharashtra RERA guidelines.
