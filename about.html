Who We Are

Pioneers in Real Estate Barter Construction Models

Founded in Mumbai, Barter Constructions was established to solve the critical cash flow issues faced by real estate builders and developers. Traditionally, construction requires heavy upfront capital, stalling potential projects due to liquidity issues. We revolutionized this by introducing an inventory-for-services model.

We execute full RCC core construction, shell development, building material supplies, and premium finishes. Instead of demanding liquid payments, we accept completed or semi-completed residential flats, shops, and office spaces at negotiated valuations.

Our Services

Why Choose Our Model?

  • Retain Cash flow: Builders save up to 40% of standard liquid cash outlays.
  • High-quality RCC: Strict adherence to BIS concrete mix grades.
  • RERA Compliance: Clean documentation and legal escrow matching.
  • Fast Execution: Avoid project delays due to liquidity freezes.
